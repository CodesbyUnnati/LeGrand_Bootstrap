About Us

Established in the year 2010 with a motive to provide professional services in the field of HR solutions, management, travel, hospitality and real estate, Le grand has guided its customers throughout the years, irrespective of their work profile. Our professionals decided to undertake a preliminary action to transform Le grand into a full time HR consultancy, in order to provide reliable human resource services, in 2012. We assist the recruiting sectors with digital solutions while sourcing and hiring, which makes us distinguishable.

Our Mission

Our main focus lies in encouraging the collaboration with the hiring teams, to reduce the wastage of time and resources while recruiting, thereby improving the candidate's experience in finding relevant jobs. Expertising our client's skills, improving their employability and preparing them for all the challenges during recruitment are the main functions we are acknowledged for.

Our Vision

Here at Le grand, we strongly believe that not only technical knowledge but soft skill is also important for an individual to grow. Nowadays employers seek candidates who fit into the company's culture, here is when the soft skills become mandatory. Our team of experts put forward their knowledge and skills to provide great training sessions to the clients.

Our Plan

The main purpose behind providing HR consultancy services is to assist every individual to get them into their dream organisation. We are committed to accomplish our passion to serve the freshers with all the needful during the recruitments. Our whole team work willingly, with the determination to support the clients throughout their learning process.
